Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Chopped onion
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, breadcrumbs and chopped onion.
  3. Form into a loaf and place on a pan. Smother the top with ketchup.
  4. Bake in oven for 1.5 hours.
