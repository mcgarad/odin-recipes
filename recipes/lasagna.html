Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese
  • ground beef
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown ground beef.
  3. Layer noodles, cheese, beef and sauce in a deep pan.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour.
